Our Mission

Education shouldn't depend on where you come from.

Codara was built with one belief — that every student deserves access to great learning resources, regardless of budget, location, or background. We built a platform where the community curates the best educational content from across the web, combines it with powerful study tools, and makes the whole thing completely free. No catch. No subscription. Just learning.
